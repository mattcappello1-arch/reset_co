FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work after hours?+
Yes — most of our hospitality clients prefer overnight or early morning cleans. We're available 7 days and can work around any schedule, including last-minute requests.
Are there lock-in contracts?+
No lock-in contracts. We work on flexible arrangements because we believe our quality should be reason enough to stay. Most clients do — but you're never locked in.
What areas do you service?+
We cover the Yarra Valley and surrounding areas including Healesville, Coldstream, Yering, Seville, and Warburton. Contact us if you're unsure whether we reach your venue.
What products do you use?+
We use professional-grade, eco-conscious products that meet Australian food safety and health standards. Safe for guests and the environment.
How quickly can you start?+
After the venue walk and program agreement, most clients are up and running within a week. For urgent requirements we can often move faster — just ask.
