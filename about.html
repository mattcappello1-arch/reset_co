Who We Are

Cleaning is the first
impression you never see.

Reset Co was founded by hospitality people, for hospitality people. We grew up working in restaurants, resorts and spas across the region — and knew better than anyone that the invisible work of cleaning is what makes every guest experience possible.

We built this business because hospitality venues in the Yarra Valley deserved better than a generic commercial cleaning service. They deserved a team that understood their industry's rhythms — the late nights, the early mornings, the meticulous standards.

Today we work with some of the region's finest restaurants, spas, accommodation providers and cellar doors. Every client gets a bespoke program built around their venue, their schedule, and their guests.
